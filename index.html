About Andy



Hi, I'm Andy Sagar. How are you?

I write stories, mostly for children (and for grown-ups who feel like they could use an adventure now and again). My favourite month is October and my favourite animal is the tortoise. I was raised in Yorkshire but now live between London, where I write, and Cambridge, where I study how law relates to witchcraft.

When I was in school, I wanted to be an author, or a baker, or an octopus expert, or a fortune teller. At least one of these ambitions has now been fulfilled (I’m still waiting to hear back on my application to fortune teller school).

To reach me, please see my Contact Me page.

'Yesterday Crumb and the Storm in a Teacup'
Coming 2022!



When a girl named Yesterday discovers a magical, walking teashop run by the flamboyant tea witch Miss Dumpling, she believes her dreams of adventure have finally come true. But dark forces linger in the shadows, and she soon must race against time to defeat an ancient, evil force, or the teashop and its wondrous magic will be snatched away forever...
